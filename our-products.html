About Eloria

A legacy of excellence in flavours and fragrances, serving premium brands worldwide

Eloria is a leading manufacturer and supplier of premium flavours and fragrances, dedicated to transforming everyday experiences into extraordinary sensory journeys. With decades of expertise in sensory science and formulation chemistry, we partner with food, beverage, cosmetics, and lifestyle brands to create distinctive products that captivate and inspire.

Our state-of-the-art facilities, combined with our commitment to innovation and sustainability, enable us to deliver consistent, high-quality solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients across the globe.

Our Mission

To elevate everyday experiences through world-class flavours & fragrances, empowering brands to create products that resonate deeply with consumers.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in sensory innovation, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer excellence.

  • Innovation
  • Quality
  • Sustainability
  • Integrity

Global Presence
